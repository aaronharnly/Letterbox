Letterbox 0.24b6

Released March 10, 2009

This release fixes some annoying bugs, and brings a friendly installer. Long awaited — thanks for your patience, your support, and your friendly prods to get me developing Letterbox again!

Details:

Includes a friendly installer.
Message list and preview pane sizes are now remembered between launches.
Letterbox no longer fails to load under some circumstances.
Still incompatible with some Mail plugins that also use Sparkle for auto-updating.
The two-line combined From/Subject column doesn't play well with fonts other than Helvetica and Lucida Grande.
Changing the preview pane position with the menu item or keyboard shortcut does not visibly change the value in the preference pane.
